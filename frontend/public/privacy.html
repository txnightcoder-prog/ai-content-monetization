Privacy Policy

KidVid Creator  ·  Last updated: June 2025

COPPA Compliant

KidVid Creator ("we", "us", or "our") operates the KidVid Creator platform (the "Service"), which produces personalized AI-generated children's videos for parents and content creators. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service.

1. Who We Are

KidVid Creator is an AI-powered video creation service. The Service is operated by the creator of the platform. If you have questions, contact us at: txnightcoder@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to create your personalized video:

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you place an order.
  • Order details: Child's name, age range, hair colour, eye colour, and any additional personalisation features you provide. This information is used solely to generate your video.
  • Payment information: Payments are processed by Gumroad. We do not store credit card or payment details on our servers.
  • Usage data: Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) for security and performance monitoring.
  • Social media metrics: If you connect your TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, or Facebook accounts, we read your publicly available post statistics (views, likes, comments, shares) to display them in your dashboard. We do not post on your behalf without explicit action from you.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and deliver your personalised video.
  • To send you the finished video download link by email.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To display your social media performance metrics in your dashboard.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising profiling.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

KidVid Creator is a business-to-creator service. The users of the Service are adult content creators and parents — not children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13.

Child personalisation details (name, age, appearance) provided at order time are used exclusively to generate the requested video and are deleted from our active systems within 90 days of order completion.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at txnightcoder@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Gumroad — payment processing (Gumroad Privacy Policy)
  • TikTok API — reading video performance metrics for connected accounts (TikTok Privacy Policy)
  • Microsoft Azure — cloud hosting and storage (Microsoft Privacy Statement)
  • OpenAI — AI script and prompt generation (OpenAI Privacy Policy)
  • ElevenLabs — AI voiceover generation (ElevenLabs Privacy Policy)
  • SendGrid / Mailgun — email delivery

6. Data Retention

  • Order records and associated personalisation data: retained for 90 days after delivery, then deleted.
  • Generated video files: retained for 30 days after delivery, then deleted from our servers.
  • Analytics data synced from social platforms: retained indefinitely unless you request deletion.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, email txnightcoder@gmail.com.

8. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit and at rest. Access to production systems is restricted to authorised personnel only.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
